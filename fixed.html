BRIDGE

A GALACTIC PROJECT

EDWIN HUBBLE studied the light coming from galaxies in our night sky.

He discovered that, due to results from the
DOPPLER EFFECT, the redshifts in special class
stars stems from the fact that galaxies are moving
farther from us, and are expanding the diameter
of the universe at great speed.

This is now known as Hubble’s Law.
